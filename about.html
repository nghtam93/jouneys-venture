A Pioneer in Crypto Investments

J Ventures, founded in 2016 in Amsterdam (Netherlands), is a fund manager that offers qualified investors to gain exposure and participate in the emerging crypto asset class with professional, secure, liquid, and transparent investment services.

The company was among the first worldwide to launch a cryptocurrency fund in 2016 and holds a successful track record in generating superior returns for a long-term investment horizon. J Ventures approaches crypto asset management with the same professional mind-set and standards as present in the traditional financial industry.

As of April 1, 2021, J Ventures partners with Sensus Capital, an independent services company based in Zug, Switzerland with the goal to combine established crypto investment expertise with traditional investment business and network to further enhance the added value for clients.

J Ventures strongly believes in finding the right balance between crypto-native expertise and the institutional financial marketplace. The team combines a crypto-native expertise with a honed investment strategy to create smart investment opportunities in a digital economy.
